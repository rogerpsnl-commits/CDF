Who We Are

A Miami firm with deep roots, global reach, and a commitment to the community we serve.

Our Story

Born in Miami, Built for Your Success

Chabo, De Feria & Co. was founded with a clear mission: to provide Miami's growing and diverse business community with accounting services that match the sophistication of the city itself. We saw a need for a firm that could bridge cultures, languages, and industries - and we built CDF to fill it.

From our office near Doral, we serve clients across Miami-Dade County and beyond - from Brickell high-rises to Homestead agricultural operations, from Wynwood startups to established enterprises throughout Latin America. Our three partners bring a combined 60+ years of public and private accounting experience, all grounded in the unique business environment of South Florida.

We are proud members of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and the Florida Institute of Certified Public Accountants (FICPA), and we hold ourselves to the highest professional and ethical standards.
